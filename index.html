Introduction

Welcome to P.C.E.A Makupa Parish, a place where faith and community come together to create a profound spiritual journey. We have been a steadfast presence in the lives of our congregation, nurturing and fostering spiritual growth.


As we continue to evolve and adapt to the changing world, our commitment to spreading the message of love and hope remains unwavering. We invite you to explore our website, join our services, and be part of our thriving community. Together, we can strengthen our faith and make a positive impact in the world.

JOIN US LIVE

Experience the beauty of our worship services from the comfort of your home. Join us online as we come together in praise, prayer, and community. Our virtual services offer a unique opportunity to connect with our church family and feel the presence of God.


We invite you to join us on our YouTube channel, where you can watch our services, access archived sermons, and stay connected with our church community. It's a platform where faith knows no boundaries, and we welcome you to be a part of this digital congregation.
