Our Core Values

1. We believe in the Holy Trinity, the Supremacy of the Word of God as the truth and application of the word of God through sound doctrine
2. Prayer is central to all that we do and we are committed to prayer and fellowship to nurture spiritual growth
3. We up hold and extend the love of God to one another and even those who are not of our church
4.We emulate the humility of Christ
5. We are mission oriented church focusing not only inside but also outside of our physical administrative boundaries, to reach many through ecumenical partnerships
6. We believe in excellence through honesty, integrity, accountability, and responsible use of our gifts for the service of Christ
7. We believe in strong, loving family units that are monogamous and heterosexual
8. We believe in inclusivity and the participation of all members according to the gifts given by God
9. We believe in nurturing the youth and children to uphold Christian values and safeguard the future of our fellowship


Vision

To build a strong fellowship of Christian believers by proclaiming and teaching the Good News of the risen Christ to all people


Mission

To be a Christian institution that transforms lives through biblical knowledge and empowers members wellbeing in education, health, and poverty alleviation through evangelism.
